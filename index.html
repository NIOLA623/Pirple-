How to prepare my favorite meal: Spaghetti and Meatballs

Today I am going to explain how to prepare Spaghetti and Meatballs

The Ingredients required:

  • A pack of Spaghetti
  • Bell Pepper flakes
  • Can crushed tomatoes
  • Onions, finely chopped
  • Vegetable oil
  • 1 Egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Crushed Bay leaf
  • Seasonings
    • Maggi
    • Salt
    • Curry
    • Thyme
  • Ground beef
  • Vegetables as desired

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook Spaghetti according to package instructions
  2. In a large bowl, combine beef with bread crumbs,eeg, garlic,red Pepper flakes. Mix until just combined into 10 balls
  3. In a large pot over medium heat,heat oil. Add meatballs and cook turning ocassionally, until brown on all sides,about 10mins.transfer meat ball to a plate
  4. Add onion to pot and cook until soft. Add crushed tomatoes and bay leaf
  5. Season with salt and pepper and bring to simmer, return meatballs to pot and cover. Simmer until sauce has thickened, 8-10mins
  6. Serve pasta with healthy scoop of meatballs and sauce.
